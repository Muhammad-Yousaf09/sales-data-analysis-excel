# 📊 AtliQ Hardware — Business Intelligence Reports

## 🏢 About AtliQ Hardware

AtliQ Hardware is a global hardware company offering a diverse portfolio of products — from **personal computers and laptops** to **mice, printers, and peripheral devices**. Serving both individual consumers and enterprise clients, AtliQ manufactures its own products and distributes them through strategic retail and digital partners including **Croma**, **Best Buy**, **Flipkart**, and **Amazon**.

These partnerships bridge the gap between AtliQ's manufacturing capabilities and millions of end consumers worldwide — making channel strategy a critical lever for growth.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Despite strong product demand, AtliQ lacked a centralized view of its sales and financial performance across markets, divisions, and product lines. Decision-makers had no reliable way to benchmark growth, spot underperformers, or measure against targets.

**The goal:** Build an interactive **Sales & Finance Analytics Report** that transforms raw transactional data into clear, actionable intelligence — empowering leadership to make faster, smarter, data-driven decisions.

---

## 📦 Sales Analysis

### 🎯 Objectives
- Identify the **Top 10 products** with the highest net sales growth from 2020 to 2021
- Generate a **Division-level sales report** with YoY revenue and growth %
- Rank the **Top 5 and Bottom 5 products** by units sold
- Surface all **new products** introduced in 2021
- Pinpoint the **Top 5 countries** driving net sales in 2021

## 💰 Financial Analysis

### 🎯 Objectives
- Deliver a **Customer Net Sales Performance** overview
- Compare **Market Performance vs. Sales Targets**
- Produce **Yearly and Quarterly P&L reports**
- Analyze **Market-specific P&L** for regional profitability
- Track **Gross Margin % (GM%) by Quarter and Geography**

---

### 📊 Financial Reports & Key Insights

#### 💼 Customer Net Sales Performance
> Revenue trajectory across all customer segments.

| Fiscal Year | Net Sales | Growth |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| 2019 | $87.5M | — |
| 2020 | $196.7M | +124.8% |
| 2021 | $598.9M | **+204.5%** |

💡 *AtliQ grew net sales by over **585% in just two years** — a testament to aggressive market expansion and product diversification.*

---

#### 🎯 Market Performance vs. Sales Targets (2021)
> All markets fell short of their 2021 targets — but the gap reveals strategic priorities.

- **Overall shortfall:** -$54.9M (-9.2%) below target
- **Poland** missed by the widest margin at **-18.1%**
- **Portugal** came closest to target, missing by just **-4.3%**
- **India**, despite the highest absolute shortfall (-$9.6M), performed best proportionally at **-5.9%**

💡 *Missing targets isn't failure — it's data. These gaps highlight where sales infrastructure, channel investment, or marketing needs to be strengthened.*

---

#### 📅 Profit & Loss — By Fiscal Year

| Metric | 2019 | 2020 | 2021 |
|--------|------|------|------|
| Net Sales | $87.5M | $196.7M | $598.9M |
| COGS | $51.2M | $123.4M | $380.7M |
| Gross Margin | $36.2M | $73.3M | $218.2M |
| **GM %** | **41.4%** | **37.3%** | **36.4%** |

💡 *While revenue is scaling rapidly, the declining GM% (41.4% → 36.4%) signals rising costs. Optimizing the cost structure should be a priority for 2022 planning.*
